About

Devonshire Lending is a regional savings and loan in Pittsburgh and has had success with a number of niche lending products.

This OBS aims to increase efficiency by automating customer-facing processes, reduce overhead by reducing the number of brick and mortar branches, and expand the market outside of the Devonshire region.

In this system, you can do nearly everything that you are served in a local branch such as check account balance, transfer funds, pay bills, etc.
